Proposition 2.2.5. Bezout Identity.

If \(a,b\in \Z\) then there exists \(x,y\in \Z\) so that \(ax+by = \gcd(a,b)\text{.}\)

🔗

Hint.

Try a constructive direct proof. Use the Euclidean Algorithm to find the gcd and then work backwards to find the coefficients. You may find Proposition 2.2.4 useful.
