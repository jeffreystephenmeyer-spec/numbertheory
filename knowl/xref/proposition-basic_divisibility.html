Proposition 2.1.2. Basic divisibilities.

If \(a\in \Z\) where \(a\ne 0\text{,}\) then
  1. \(\displaystyle 1 \mid a \)
    🔗
    🔗
  2. \(\displaystyle -1 \mid a \)
    🔗
    🔗
  3. \(\displaystyle a \mid 0 \)
    🔗
    🔗
🔗
🔗
